Getting Started

Welcome to AndRPi! Follow these steps to set up your device:

  1. Unbox your AndRPi tablet or prepare your own Raspberry Pi.
  2. Insert your pre-installed MicroSD card or install the AndRPi OS on your own card.
  3. Follow the on-screen setup instructions to configure your tablet.
